About Us

A VISION UNFOLDED

Cinema Factory Kids Academy helps children explore the art of storytelling through film. With expert mentors, fun workshops, and creative projects, we make learning cinema an unforgettable experience! Our mission is to inspire young creators and nurture their love for movies in a playful and engaging way.


In 2021, Rajesh Ravindran envisioned Cinema Factory as a transformative force in the film industry, rooted in innovation and strategic collaboration. What began as an ambitious idea quickly gained momentum, evolving into a thriving hub for creativity and industry advancement.
